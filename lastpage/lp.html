Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How can I register to vote online?

Ans: To register, go to the "Login/Register" section and follow the instructions for creating a new account. Provide the required information and complete the registration process.

Q2: What information is required for voter registration?

Ans: Voter registration may require personal details such as your name, mobile number, address, and a unique identifier. Follow the prompts during registration for specific requirements.

Q3: Can I change my vote after submitting it?

Ans: No, once a vote is submitted, it cannot be changed. Make sure to review your choices before confirming your vote.

Q4: How is the privacy of my vote ensured?

Ans: We implement robust security measures, including encryption and secure transmission, to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of each vote cast. Your choices are confidential and secure.

Q5: What if I forget my login credentials?

Ans: You can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email to regain access to your account.

About Us

Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information during the registration process, including names, mobile numbers, and addresses. This information is used solely for the purpose of facilitating the online voting process and is stored securely.

2. Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect the confidentiality and integrity of user data. This includes encryption, secure login procedures, and regular system audits. Users are encouraged to choose strong passwords and keep their login credentials confidential.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties unless required by law. User data is used exclusively for the functioning of the Online Voting System. Aggregate, non-personal data may be used for analytical purposes.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on the user's device that help us analyze usage patterns and improve the platform. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.
